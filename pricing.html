Pricing Information

How Our Laundry Pricing Works

Every order is unique, which is why some garments may cost more than others. Our pricing is based on the care each item requires, ensuring your clothes receive the most appropriate cleaning treatment while keeping our pricing transparent and fair.

Garment Type

Different garments require different cleaning methods. Everyday clothing, business suits, dresses, native wear, bedding, and delicate garments each have their own cleaning requirements.

Fabric Type

Cotton, linen, wool, silk, lace, and other delicate fabrics require different handling techniques to help maintain their appearance and longevity.

Special Care Requirements

Heavy stains, delicate embellishments, wedding garments, curtains, rugs, and specialty fabrics may require additional treatment and therefore attract different pricing.

Bulk & Commercial Orders

Businesses, hotels, Airbnb hosts, restaurants, spas, gyms, and other commercial customers can request custom quotations based on laundry volume and collection schedules.
