About Foldora

We're helping busy professionals, families, and businesses across Abuja enjoy clean clothes without the stress of doing laundry themselves.

Our story

Making Professional Laundry Simple, Convenient & Reliable

Foldora was created to help busy professionals, families, and businesses spend less time worrying about laundry and more time focusing on what matters most. By combining convenient pickup and delivery with professional garment care, we make keeping clothes clean simple and stress-free.

Every order follows a careful process. Garments are inspected, sorted according to their fabric and care requirements, cleaned using appropriate methods, professionally finished, and checked before delivery. This attention to detail helps us care for everything from everyday clothing and office wear to bedding, delicate fabrics, and commercial laundry.

Whether you need weekly household laundry, dry cleaning for important garments, or recurring laundry services for your business, Foldora is committed to providing dependable service, transparent pricing, and a consistently professional experience from pickup to delivery.

Our process

Our Cleaning Standards

Every garment follows a consistent process designed to help protect fabrics while delivering fresh, professionally cleaned clothes every time.

Inspection

Every item is inspected before cleaning to identify stains, delicate fabrics, care labels, and any special handling requirements.

Sorting

Clothes are separated according to fabric type and cleaning requirements to ensure each garment receives appropriate care.

Professional Cleaning

Suitable cleaning methods are selected to help preserve fabric quality while removing dirt, odours, and stains.

Quality Check

Before delivery, garments are inspected, neatly finished, folded or hung appropriately, and carefully packaged.
